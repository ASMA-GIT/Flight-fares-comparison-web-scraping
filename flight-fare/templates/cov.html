Covid Guidelines during Flight travel

Passengers good health and safety is very essestial for us.It is important to act in accordance with all the safety, hygiene, and sanitization guidelines

    Guidelines for Domestic passengers :

  • 1. Different state governments have established their own set of guidelines and regulations for domestic travellers arriving at their airports.
  • 2. Before you travel, please read and understand the guidelines of the respective state/city you will be visiting, and follow them to make your trip arrangements easier.
  • Note: State restrictions are subject to change; please check the state government website for more information.

    General Guidelines for Domestic Air Travel :

  • 1. Before entering the airport terminal, passengers are requested to wear a face mask as per prevailing regulations. They will continue to wear the mask throughout their journey until they exit the airport
  • 2. Passengers are also encouraged to use a face shield for additional protection
  • 3. Passengers are advised not to travel and quarantine themselves if they exhibit symptoms
  • 4. Passengers may be fined for breaking COVID-19 protocols or not wearing a mask
  • 5. Passengers are requested to maintain social distancing
  • 6. During arrival and departure at the airport terminal, passengers can sanitise their bags as per the availability of sanitization machines at the airports
