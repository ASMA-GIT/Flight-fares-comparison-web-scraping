*Provisons & Hidden Offers

Student Travel Offers

Students can avail discounts on all domestic flights, students who are 12 years and above, need to provide a valid ID card issued by the school or university while checking in at the airport.

Flight Lounges check-in

Now you can get access to airport lounges on domestic and international flights. Some of the bank credit cards offer free access to lounges and food or with minimum charges.

Offers for Senior Citizens

Senior citizens who want to travel can avail discounts on all domestic flights, people who are 60 years and above, need to provide a valid ID card and identity proofs to avail discounts.
